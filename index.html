Dr. Allama Muhammad Iqbal

The Poet of the East

Here's a time line of Dr. Allama Muhammad Iqbal's life:

  • 1877 - Born in Sialkot, Pakistan
  • 1899 - Completes Master in Arts from Govt. College of Lahore
  • 1908 - Completed his P.hd from Germany
  • 1922 - King George V of Britain, made him a knight, giving him the title of Allama 'Sir' Muhammad Iqbal.
  • 1927 - He joined the Muslim League in January 1927.
  • 1930 -Iqbal was invited to preside over the open session of the Muslim League at Allahabad. In his historic Allahabad Address, Iqbal visualised an independent and sovereign state for the Muslims of North-Western India.
  • 1938 - He died on 21 april 1938 due to throat infection.
